Design-O-Thon

Poster Design Competition
RULES

Participants would be required to mail their entries. The entries would be shortlisted for the final round (on campus round).

Shortlisted participants would be provided with 2 options: 1st option would require them to bring their own laptop and dongle to the campus and design a poster on the topic assigned, on the spot. 2nd option would allow them to design the poster(on the topic assigned) at their homes, but they'll be required to submit it before the prescribed deadline and they would have to present their poster to the judges, talking about the theme, concept and the artistic approach used.
