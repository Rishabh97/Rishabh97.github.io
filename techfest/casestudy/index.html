Stratagem-The Case Competition

Case Study Competition
RULES

A case study would be mailed to all the participants and they would be required to mail back their solutions before the prescribed deadline.

Shortlisiting would be done, based on their solutions. The selected teams would be mailed another case and they would be required to present their solution to that case on the event day in front of the judges. The winners would be decided on the basis of scoring done by the judges.
